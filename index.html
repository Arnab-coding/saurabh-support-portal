Request Letter

To
The Head of the Department
Department of Psychiatry
Pavlov Hospital, Kolkata

Subject: Request for continued inpatient care of Dr. Saurabh Ghosh

Respected Sir/Madam,

We, the undersigned batchmates of Dr. Saurabh Ghosh, respectfully submit this letter in support of his continued inpatient care at Pavlov Hospital.

We understand that Dr. Saurabh Ghosh is currently undergoing treatment for chronic schizophrenia under your care.

At present, he does not have a legal guardian or caregiver who is available to assume responsibility for his care outside the hospital.

We therefore earnestly request that Dr. Saurabh Ghosh may kindly be allowed to continue receiving inpatient treatment at Pavlov Hospital until such time as a suitable legal guardian or responsible caregiver becomes available.

Although we are not his legal guardians and cannot assume legal responsibility for his treatment or discharge, we remain deeply concerned about his well-being.

We assure you that we shall continue to remain in touch with the hospital authorities and extend all possible cooperation and assistance whenever required in the future regarding his care and rehabilitation.

We sincerely appreciate the dedicated services rendered by your department and request you to kindly consider this appeal in the best interest of Dr. Saurabh Ghosh.

Thank you.

Yours faithfully,

The Undersigned Batchmates
